IRCTC Simplified Login

I redesigned the IRCTC Railway Connect app's login and sign-up process by creating a minimalistic and decluttered UI. I streamlined the confusing login security process and transformed the complex, single-page sign-up form into a simple, four-step process with only 4-5 fields per step. Additionally, I added an intuitive "forgot password" feature, significantly enhancing the overall user experience.
